Stress Management Techniques

Here are some techniques you can try to manage stress:

  • Deep Breathing Exercises
  • Meditation and Mindfulness
  • Physical Activity and Exercise
  • Journaling
  • Talking to a trusted friend or therapist

Support Groups in Your Area

Connecting with others can be very beneficial. Here are some resources:

  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)
  • Psychology Today's Therapy Groups

Emergency Contacts

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out for help. Here are some contacts:

  • Emergency Services: 911
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741
